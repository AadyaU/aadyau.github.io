1. Hangman-Style App

I coded this app during my Intro to App Design dual enrollment class. It was made on Xcode, using Swift. When you play the game, the apples appear in the tree and the labels are replaced with the mystery word and your win/loss statistic You use the keyboard to select the letters to guess the word, and have 5 guesses. For every letter you guess wrong, an apple falls from the tree.

4. Meal Planner Program

The Meal Planner program is designed to simplify and organize meal preparation, helping users create balanced, customized daily menus based on dietary preferences and needs. This program provides tools to save time, stay organized, and promote healthier eating habits.

5. BudgetBite App Presentation (Won 3rd Place Internationally)

BudgetBite is a community-focused app designed to help users save money on food while supporting small, family-owned, and women-owned businesses. Perfect for anyone living on a budget or simply looking to cut costs, BudgetBite helps users locate affordable meal options, such as marked-down or discounted items, and find coupons to make meals more affordable. With rising costs in the economy, BudgetBite aims to address issues of food insecurity by connecting people in need with low-cost, accessible meal options that fit within their budget.

6. Cipher Encoder and Decoder

In GWC SPP, I coded the algorithm for a cipher to encrypt and decrypt messages. It takes in your message, asks you the key, and whether you are encrypting or decrypting your message. Once you enter all the information, it displays your encrypted or decrypted message.
